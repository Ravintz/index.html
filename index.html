For the first step we need to now, what is Elden Ring?

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was released in February 2022 and has received critical acclaim for its open-world design, intricate lore, and challenging gameplay.

Radahn The “Starscourge”

Radahn's story is intertwined with themes of honor, madness, and destiny, making him a central figure in the game's narrative.

Malenia

Malenia, Blade of Miquella, is a pivotal character in Elden Ring, known for her tragic backstory and formidable combat skills. She is the twin sister of Miquella and a key figure in the game's lore.

Morgott

Morgott, the Omen King, is a complex character in Elden Ring, known for his tragic backstory and his role as a guardian of the Erdtree. He is a pivotal figure in the game's lore, embodying themes of loyalty and sacrifice.
